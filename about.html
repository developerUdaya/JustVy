Our Journey with DST,
Govt Of India

We are proud to announce that we have secured funding from the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India, under the NewGen IEDC (Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Center) initiative. This support empowers us to drive innovation, develop cutting-edge solutions, and make a significant impact in the tech industry. As a startup, we are dedicated to software development, app development, web development, machine learning, and IoT, and this funding further fuels our mission to bring about positive change.
